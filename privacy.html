Privacy Policy

Last updated — 15 July 2025

At MONIPAY, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By visiting our website or using our apps, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: name, email, phone, username, password (hashed).
  • Usage data: pages visited, tasks completed, balances, referral clicks.
  • Device data: browser, operating system, IP address (for security).
  • Optional details: profile photo, withdrawal bank details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the MONIPAY services.
  • Calculate earnings, referrals, and task progress.
  • Send transactional emails, push, or WhatsApp updates.
  • Improve our platform through analytics and feedback.
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and abuse.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser cookies and localStorage to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and store task progress on static pages. You can clear or block cookies, but some features may not work.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted providers such as Firebase (Auth & Firestore), Formspree (contact form), and analytics tools. These partners only process data on our behalf and under strict privacy terms.

5. Data Retention

We keep your account data as long as your profile is active or as needed to comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time (see “Your Rights”).

6. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest (AES-256 in Firebase). We monitor our systems for vulnerabilities and limit access to employees on a need-to-know basis.

7. Children’s Privacy

MONIPAY is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have, we delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct, update, or delete that data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

Email monipay817@gmail.com to submit a request.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We’ll post the new version on this page and notify you via email or channel announcements if the changes are significant.

10. Contact Us

Questions or concerns? Reach us at monipay817@gmail.com or via our Contact Us page.
